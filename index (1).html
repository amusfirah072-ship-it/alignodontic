Common questions

Everything you
need to know

01What are clear aligners?+

Clear aligners are custom-made, removable, virtually invisible trays that gently shift your teeth into their ideal position — smooth, comfortable, and designed for modern life.

02How long does treatment take?+

Most treatments take 6–18 months depending on complexity. Your consultation provides a precise timeline from your personalised digital plan.

03Can I eat while wearing aligners?+

Remove aligners before all meals. Eat whatever you like with zero restrictions, then brush, rinse and reinsert. It's that simple.

04How should I clean my aligners?+

Rinse with lukewarm water and gently brush with a soft toothbrush after meals. Avoid hot water or coloured soaps to protect the material.

05Will aligners affect my speech?+

A very minor adjustment of 1–3 days is typical. Any slight lisp disappears quickly as your tongue naturally adapts to the ultra-thin trays.

06How many hours should I wear them?+

20–22 hours per day for best results. Remove only for eating, drinking (except water) and brushing. Consistency is everything.
